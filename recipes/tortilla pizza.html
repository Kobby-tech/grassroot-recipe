Tortilla-pizza

Description

A flat, open baked pie of Italian origin A thin layer of bread dough topped with tamato sauce and cheese

Often garnished with sausage slices, anchovies, mushrooms, etc.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour Tortilla
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • ½ cup mozzarella
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese
  • 24 slices pepperoni
  • garlic salt(optional)

Steps

  1. Prep your oven and your dough. Preheat your oven first. It should be set to 218°C
  2. Add your toppings
  3. Bake, slice, and serve
