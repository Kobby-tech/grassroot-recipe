Egg noodle

Description

Egg noodle is a term used to describe a noodle made with egg.

They have high protein content and a high rate of ater absorption.

Ingredients

  • 250gm parboiled fresh noodles
  • 1 cup chopped, grated cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrot
  • 1 cup sliced onion
  • 7 sliced green chilli
  • 2 teaspoon powderedblack pepper
  • 2 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 3 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ajinomoto
  • 1 cup finely chopped bean
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
    • For garnishing
  • 1 handfulls chopped coriander leaves.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles. Once done drain the excess waterand keep it inside.
  2. Heat pan over a medium flame. Once the oil is hot, add the ginger-garlic paste and fry for some time.
  3. Toss the vggies and cook the egg
  4. Add the onions, beans and green chillies in the same pan and saute for a minute or two
  5. Add the carrots, cabbage, salt, pepper powder, soy sauce and Ajinomoto
  6. Stir-fry the ingredients for some time.
  7. Add the boiled noodles and break the eggs into a pan. Mix well and cook for a few minutes
  8. Stir the viggies mix on a high flame
  9. Cover the pan with a lid and allow it to cook for around 2-3 minutes.
  10. Once done transfer the dish to a serving bowl and garnish it with coriander leaves.
